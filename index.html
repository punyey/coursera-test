OUR MENU

CHICKEN

To make chicken, start by seasoning your preferred cut with salt, pepper, and any other spices you enjoy. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat, then add the chicken and cook until it's browned on both sides. Reduce the heat, cover the pan, and let the chicken cook through, about 15-20 minutes depending on the thickness. You can check if it's done by using a meat thermometer to ensure it reaches an internal temperature of 165°F (74°C). Once cooked, you can enjoy the chicken as is or use it in your favorite recipes.

BEEF

Season your beef with salt, pepper, and any other desired spices. Heat oil in a pan over medium-high heat. Sear the beef on all sides until well browned, then reduce the heat to medium. For steaks, cook to your desired doneness using a meat thermometer (145°F for medium-rare, 160°F for medium, 170°F for well-done). For roasts, cook low and slow until tender. Let the beef rest for a few minutes before slicing against the grain.

SUSHI

Making sushi starts with perfectly cooked and seasoned sushi rice. While the rice cools, prepare your fillings, such as sliced fish, vegetables, or other ingredients. Lay a sheet of nori seaweed on a bamboo mat, spread a thin layer of rice on top, and arrange your chosen fillings in the center. Using the mat, carefully roll the nori and rice around the filling to form a tight cylinder. Finally, slice the roll into bite-sized pieces and serve with soy sauce, wasabi, and ginger.
